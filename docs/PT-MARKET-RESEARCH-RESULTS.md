# Dutch Personal Trainer Market Research
**Date:** 2026-02-17
**Researcher:** Ava Sterling (Claude Researcher)
**For:** Bollenstreek Digitaal

---

## Executive Summary

This research analyzes the Dutch personal trainer (PT) market from a website/digital services perspective. The market is fragmented with opportunities for Bollenstreek Digitaal to position as a local, Dutch-speaking, all-inclusive provider at competitive rates.

**Key Finding:** Most Dutch PTs face a choice between expensive all-in-one software (EUR 79-105+/mo) or DIY website builders (EUR 17-39/mo) without PT-specific features. There's a gap for affordable, professionally-built websites with integrated booking - exactly what BD can offer.

## 6. Gap Analysis & BD Opportunities

### What's Missing in the Market

1. **Affordable All-in-One for Solo PTs**
   - Software providers (Virtuagym, SportBit) are expensive and don't include websites
   - Website builders (Wix, Squarespace) don't include PT-specific booking/admin
   - Gap: Combined solution at EUR 50-99/mo

2. **Dutch Language + Local Support**
   - Wix/Squarespace: English support, no iDEAL native
   - International software: Often English-first
   - Gap: True Dutch support with local presence

3. **Bollenstreek Local Positioning**
   - No local agency specifically targets PTs
   - Existing PTs (BodyBouw, FunFit, Lisbox) have functional but not outstanding websites
   - Gap: "Your neighbor's web partner who understands fitness"

4. **Results-Based Guarantee**
   - Personal Fitness Nederland claims "100% results guarantee, money back"
   - No web agency makes similar promises
   - Gap: "If we don't deliver X, you don't pay"

### BD Competitive Advantages

| Advantage | Opportunity |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Local presence** | Face-to-face meetings in Noordwijk/Lisse/Katwijk |
| **Dutch native** | No language barriers, understands Dutch payment (iDEAL, SEPA) |
| **All-inclusive pricing** | Website + hosting + SEO + booking = one monthly fee |
| **MKB focus** | Not trying to serve enterprise, focused on solo PTs |
| **Personal service** | Not a faceless platform |

### Recommended BD Positioning

**Target:** Solo personal trainers and small PT studios in Bollenstreek (and beyond)

**Package Idea:**
| Package | Price | Includes |
|---------|-------|----------|
| PT Starter | EUR 49/mo | Professional website, hosting, SSL, basic SEO, email |
| PT Groei | EUR 79/mo | + Online booking integration, contact forms, analytics |
| PT Premium | EUR 129/mo | + SEO optimization, monthly content updates, priority support |

**Differentiators:**
1. All-inclusive (no hidden costs)
2. iDEAL + Dutch payment focus
3. Booking integration (SuperSaaS, Calendly, or custom)
4. Local support (Bollenstreek-based)
5. "Starter guarantee" - first month free if not satisfied

## 7. Strategic Insights

### Second-Order Effects to Consider

1. **PT Software Consolidation**
   - Virtuagym and others will likely add website builders
   - BD should focus on relationship/service, not just features

2. **AI Website Builders Rising**
   - Wix, Squarespace adding AI
   - Commoditizes basic websites
   - BD advantage: human strategy + local understanding

3. **Post-COVID PT Market**
   - Hybrid online/offline training is standard
   - PTs need video integration, online scheduling
   - Website must support digital products

4. **Dutch Payment Evolution**
   - iDEAL dominates, but Tikkie, Apple Pay growing
   - BD should offer flexible payment integration

### Three Moves Ahead

1. **Now:** Launch PT-specific packages at competitive rates
2. **6 months:** Build case studies with local PTs (BodyBouw partnership?)
3. **12 months:** Become "the" digital partner for Bollenstreek fitness businesses
