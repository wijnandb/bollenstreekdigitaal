### 2.2 Social Media

#### LinkedIn (Primary)

**Why LinkedIn:** B2B focus, local business owners, professional context.

**Posting Schedule:**
- **Monday:** Weekly insight/tip
- **Wednesday:** Behind-the-scenes / personal story
- **Friday:** Client win / case highlight

**Content Types:**
1. **Text posts** - Personal insights, lessons learned
2. **Carousel** - Step-by-step guides, data visualizations
3. **Video** - Quick tips, tool demos, client testimonials

**Engagement Strategy:**
- Comment on 5 local business posts daily
- Join and participate in Dutch entrepreneur groups
- Share others' content with thoughtful commentary

#### Instagram (Secondary)

**Focus:** Visual proof of work, behind-the-scenes, local connection.

**Content:**
- Before/after website transformations
- Team photos (father-son angle)
- Local Bollenstreek imagery
- Stories: day-in-the-life

#### Facebook (Local Groups)

**Strategy:** Be helpful in local business groups, don't sell directly.

**Groups to join:**
- Ondernemers Bollenstreek
- MKB [Town name] groups
- Lokale business networks

### 2.4 Video Marketing

#### Content Types

| Format | Length | Platform | Frequency |
|--------|--------|----------|-----------|
| Tutorial | 3-10 min | YouTube | Weekly |
| Quick tip | 30-60 sec | LinkedIn, IG Reels | 3x/week |
| Behind scenes | 1-3 min | Instagram Stories | Daily |
| Client testimonial | 1-2 min | All platforms | Monthly |
| Demo | 2-5 min | YouTube, Website | As needed |

#### Production Workflow (Automated)

```
1. Record long-form video (10-20 min)
   ↓
2. AI transcription (Voys Intelligence or Whisper)
   ↓
3. AI identifies key moments
   ↓
4. Auto-cut into shorts (Opus Clip or custom)
   ↓
5. Add captions automatically
   ↓
6. Schedule across platforms
```

#### Video Topics

**Tutorial Series: "Website Gezondheid"**
1. Waarom laadsnelheid ertoe doet
2. Hoe je website vindbaar wordt
3. E-mail authenticatie uitgelegd
4. HTTPS en beveiligingsheaders

## 6. Content Production System

### 6.1 Weekly Workflow

```
MONDAY
├── Review last week's metrics (30 min)
├── Plan this week's content (30 min)
└── Write blog post draft (2 hrs)

TUESDAY
├── Edit and publish blog (1 hr)
├── Create social snippets from blog (30 min)
└── Schedule week's social posts (30 min)

WEDNESDAY
├── Record video content (1 hr)
├── Edit/process video (1 hr)
└── Engagement: comment on others' posts (30 min)

THURSDAY
├── PR outreach if applicable (1 hr)
├── Lead follow-ups (1 hr)
└── Content for next week ideation (30 min)

FRIDAY
├── Week review (30 min)
├── Analytics check (30 min)
└── Weekend engagement scheduling (30 min)
```

### 6.2 Content Repurposing

```
Blog Post
    ↓
├── LinkedIn text post (excerpt)
├── LinkedIn carousel (key points)
├── Twitter thread
├── Instagram carousel
├── YouTube script (expand)
├── Email newsletter section
└── Press release angle (if newsworthy)
```
