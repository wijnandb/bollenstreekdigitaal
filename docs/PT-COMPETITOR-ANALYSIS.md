# Personal Trainer Competitor Analysis

**Date:** 2026-02-17
**For:** Bollenstreek Digitaal
**Purpose:** Competitive positioning for PT Groei Systeem offering

---

## Executive Summary

The PT market is split between **software providers** (focus on workouts/admin, no websites) and **website builders** (no PT features). **No player offers an all-inclusive solution with website + booking + payments + local support + results guarantee.**

**BD's Opportunity:** Fill this gap with "Het Groei Systeem" — all-in-one, local, guaranteed.

## Category 1: PT-Specific Software

### Global Players

| Platform | Price | What They Do | What They DON'T Do |
|----------|-------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Trainerize** | €10-225/mo | Workouts, nutrition, client app | No website, no SEO, no marketing |
| **TrueCoach** | €19-45/mo | Workout programming (best-in-class) | No website, iOS only, no Dutch |
| **My PT Hub** | €14/mo | All-in-one coaching | No website, poor support reviews |
| **Everfit** | €19-105/mo | Holistic coaching platform | No website, core features paywalled |
| **PT Distinction** | €20-90/mo | Online PT specialist | No website, limited exercise library |

**Key Insight:** All focused on coaching delivery, none build websites or help with marketing.

## SWOT Analysis: BD PT Offering

### Strengths
- ✅ All-in-one (unique in market)
- ✅ Results guarantee (5 klanten of gratis)
- ✅ Local presence (Bollenstreek)
- ✅ Dutch native (iDEAL, language, BTW knowledge)
- ✅ Done-for-you (not DIY)
- ✅ Hormozi-style value stack (high perceived value)

### Weaknesses
- ⚠️ No established PT client base yet
- ⚠️ Higher price than basic Wix/Squarespace
- ⚠️ Premium tier (app) requires development capacity
- ⚠️ Limited to Bollenstreek initially

### Opportunities
- 🎯 8.400+ PTs in Netherlands, 70% ZZP underserved
- 🎯 No competitor offers all-in-one with guarantee
- 🎯 Local case studies can dominate regional market
- 🎯 Can expand to other ZZP verticals (physiotherapists, coaches)

### Threats
- ⚡ Virtuagym could add website builder
- ⚡ Race to bottom on pricing (Wix AI)
- ⚡ Economic downturn → PTs cut costs
- ⚡ Local agencies could copy positioning
